Web Hosting

To allow your instructor to easily grade your assignments you will need a web host (remote server). What is a web host you may ask. The video below will give a quick overview to answer this question.

There are lots of web hosts out there especially for PHP. For this course, web hosting will be provided at no cost for you. Each student will have a web site of their own. To access your website, enter https://[BYU-I email username goes here].codehouse.dev/acme into your browser. For example, if my BYU-I email was vestre@byui.edu, my web site address would be https://vestre.codehouse.div/acme.
