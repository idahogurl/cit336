Late Work Policy

Work is due as specified by the course calendar and indicated in the weekly overview pages and assessment descriptions. Typically assessments are due Saturday evenings at 11pm Mountain Time (America/Boise time zone).

What is late?

Work is considered late when not submitted:

  1. on time, or
  2. in an incomplete form, or
  3. submitting the wrong assessment.

Grace Period

You have up to 4 days to turn in a late assignment for full credit, after which you will receive 50% credit. Work that is late may be submitted through the Saturday prior to the final project being due. The grace period applies to the coding assignments only and not the weekly reports, teaching presentations, and final project.

Test

Be sure to check links to videos for operability and that the correct assessment was submitted to the correct location. It is your responsibility to make sure things are correct.
