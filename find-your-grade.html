Find Your Feedback

Once your instructor reviews your code, you should receive an email notification that your merge request was either merged or closed.

To find your feedback, you need to open your merge request.

  1. Open your "acme" project in GitLab
  2. Locate the sidebar on the left and click "Merge Requests".
  3. In the right panel, the Merge Requests screen will show.
  4. Click "All" and then locate your merge request.
  5. Click on the merge request's name to open it.
  6. Once the merge request loads, you will be located in the "Discussion" tab where you'll find comments about your code.
    NOTE: When a comment is below a line of code, the comment is always only about the line above unless line numbers are referenced.
  7. Clicking on the file name will take you to the file in the "Changes" tab. The "Changes" tab shows all the files you changed and the diffs of what was added and removed. Added content is green and removed content is red.
