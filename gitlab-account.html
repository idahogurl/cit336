Setup GitLab Account

  1. Open a web browser and type "gitlab.com."
  2. Once the GitLab website loads go to the upper-right corner, click "Register."
  3. Finish the remainder of the form as directed and click "Register."
  4. You will receive a verification email shortly from GitLab. If you do not receive one, check your Spam or Junk folder.
  5. Open the email and follow the directions given.

After you create a GitLab account, open this spreadsheet and enter your username. Add a new row with your name and BYU-I username if your name is not listed. Your instructor will use this information to create your repository and web site. You will be emailed the links to both of them later

Change Notifications

  1. Log into your GitLab account. If you are already logged go to the upper-right corner and click on your profile picture.
  2. Once the menu appears click "Settings".
  3. Locate the sidebar on the left and click "Notifications".

  4. In the right panel, click on the "Global" dropdown belonging to your "acme" repository.

  5. Select the "Custom" selection and check the options other than "Close merge request", "Merged merge request", and "Failed pipeline"
