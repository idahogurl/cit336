Peer Reviewing

Preparation

  1. Make your final commit and push if you have not done so.
  2. Open the Command Palette (Ctrl+Shift+P on Windows or ⌘+Shift+P on Mac).
  3. Type "Git: Checkout to..." and press "Enter".
  4. Select "peer-review" from the list of branches.
  5. Open the Command Palette (Ctrl+Shift+P on Windows or ⌘+Shift+P on Mac).
  6. Type "Git: Merge branch" and press "Enter".
  7. Select your final project's branch.
  8. Open the Command Palette (Ctrl+Shift+P on Windows or ⌘+Shift+P on Mac).
  9. Type "Git: Push" and press "Enter".
  10. Open your "acme" project in GitLab.
  11. Click on "CI / CD" > "Jobs".
  12. The most recent deploy will be on top. If the deploy is marked as failed, contact your instructor.
  13. Send your reviewer your merge request link, remote website link, and video link (if video is completed).

How to Peer Review

  1. Download the Peer Review File (Excel)
  2. Go to their merge request to allow you to examine and comment on their code.
  3. Go to their remote website to test that their code works correctly.
Enter zero points in the corresponding cell if a video link is not provided. In NO WAY does this review affect your peer's final project grade. The review is a feedback mechanism to give your peer an idea of of what they would score if they turned in their current code.
